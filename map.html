Hollownest

Hollow Knight's map, AKA Hollownest, is an underground world composed of different biomes and cities. Through out the map you can ecounter many enemies, mask and vessel shards, fast travel spots, tram stations, hot springs, boss rooms, and more. Many areas are blocked and the player must find a secret way in or abtain a key or new ability to enter.
